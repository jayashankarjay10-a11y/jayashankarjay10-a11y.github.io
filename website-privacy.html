Website Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

Thank you for visiting the official portfolio of Jay Bro. Your privacy is important to me. This policy outlines the limited ways information is handled when you visit this website.

1. Information Collection and Use

This website is a static portfolio directory and does not actively require you to create a customer account, log in, or provide personal financial information to browse it. No direct e-commerce tracking scripts or user payment databases are executed on this domain.

2. Contacting Me

If you choose to contact me using the "Contact Me" email button (jayashankar.jay10@gmail.com), your email address and any information you provide in your message will only be used to respond to your inquiry. Your email will not be sold, shared, or added to any marketing lists without your explicit consent.

3. Hosting and Server Logs

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. When you visit this site, GitHub may collect basic, anonymous server logs, such as your IP address, browser type, and timestamp, to ensure security and optimize website performance.

4. Links to Other Sites

My website contains links to other sites (like the Google Play Store, YouTube, and Instagram). If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. I strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

5. Security Practices & Data Deletion Compliance

  • Data Encrypted in Transit: To maintain secure connection standards, this portfolio website operates exclusively over an active secure socket layer transmission protocol (HTTPS) to guarantee that all basic data transferred over the connection is fully encrypted.
  • Data Deletion Requests: Because this domain does not process, record, or retain personal user profiles, customer credentials, or financial payment records, there is no localized user database to delete. For queries regarding standard email contact traces, users can request manual erasure by contacting the support address below.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date".

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact me at: jayashankar.jay10@gmail.com.
