Website Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 30, 2026

Thank you for visiting the official portfolio of Jay Bro. Your privacy is highly important to me. This policy outlines how data elements, browser cookie parameters, and third-party utility tools are managed when you visit this website (jaybro.net).

1. Information Collection and Use

This website is fundamentally a static portfolio directory designed to showcase independent software developments. It does not actively require you to create a customer account, log in to user profiles, or submit personal financial records to browse the layout. No direct e-commerce checkout tracking scripts or user payment processing databases are executed on this domain.

2. Contacting Me

If you choose to contact me using the "Contact Me" email link (jayashankar.jay10@gmail.com), your email address and any text information you provide in your message will only be used to respond to your explicit inquiry. Your email communication trace will never be sold, shared with third-party tracking lists, or added to marketing newsletters without your explicit consent.

3. Hosting and Platform Infrastructure

This website is built over the infrastructure of GitHub Pages. When you visit this site, the host systems may collect basic, anonymous server logs, such as your masking IP address, browser user-agent string, and request timestamps, to guarantee server protection and optimize network content delivery speeds.

4. Third-Party Publisher Integrations & Cookies

Google AdSense Disclosures

To support independent game development, this platform may display programmatic web advertisements managed via Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, utilize tracking cookies to serve customized ads to users based on their previous visits to this portfolio website or other web spaces across the internet.

Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its network partners to serve relevant ad layouts. Users can review their options or opt-out of personalized target advertising arrays completely at any moment by visiting the official Google Ad Settings Portal.

Google Firebase Framework

This workspace integrates Google Firebase backend features to manage analytics triggers, monitor runtime system health, and secure system connections. Firebase configurations may automatically process anonymous system telemetry metrics (such as connection latency indicators or localized system types) strictly to optimize page loading trends and protect against tracking failures. For complete information, please consult the official Firebase Security and Privacy Guidelines.

5. Links to Other Sites & Marketplace Redirections

My website contains links navigating to external digital marketplaces (such as the official Google Play Store). If you click on a third-party marketplace link, you will be redirected away from this portfolio. All optional commercial transactions, digital item upgrades, or one-time utility purchases take place entirely off-site through the sandboxed billing engines of those store platforms. I highly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every storefront or link ecosystem you visit.

6. Security Practices & Data Deletion Compliance

  • Data Encrypted in Transit: To maintain rigid connection standards, this portfolio website operates exclusively over an active secure socket layer transmission protocol (HTTPS). This guarantees that all background data transferred across the web link environment is fully encrypted in transit using industry-vetted Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) or Transport Layer Security (TLS) cryptographic protocols.
  • Data Deletion Requests (Right to Erasure): Because this domain does not process, record, or retain persistent user profiles, customer credentials, or financial credit card payment records, there is no centralized database holding user files to delete. For tracking cookies dropped by third-party publisher networks, users can instantly exercise their right to data erasure at any time by completely clearing their local browser history and tracking cookies via their native browser settings menu.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to align with changing developer store guidelines or Google publisher network revisions. Any changes will be posted directly on this page with an updated "Effective Date" at the top.
